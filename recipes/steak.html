Seasoned cod

This dish is simple and easy to make. The distinct seasoning makes it a sure favorite. Enjoy this delicious recipe!

Ingredients

  • Cod
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Green things

Steps

  1. Heat pan
  2. Add fish and butter
  3. Add salt and seasoning
  4. Wait until fully cooked
  5. Garnish
  6. Serve!
