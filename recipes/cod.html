Cod

Plain cooked pasta served without seasoning

This dish is simple and easy to make. Just take some raw noodles and boil them in water. Once fully cooked, serve them into bowl and let cool briefly. Enjoy this delicious recipe!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Add pasta
  3. Add salt to taste
  4. Wait until fully cooked
  5. Let cool
  6. Serve!
